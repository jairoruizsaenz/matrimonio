Q&A

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please reply and let us know if you will be celebrating with us by July 15th so we can have an accurate headcount.

How do I RSVP?

You can click the RSVP section above and RSVP at any time.

What should I do if I cannot make it?

You will definitely be missed! If you cannot attend our wedding, please indicate "I am unable to attend" on your RSVP so we can plan accordingly. If you have already RSVP'd "I will attend", but find that you will not make it, Please notify us as soon as you are aware.

Are kids welcome?

As much as we love your little ones, we will only be including our direct nieces and nephew (Julieta, Irene, & Oliver) in the ceremony and reception. However, we recognize that some of you will be traveling with your kids, so please let us know if you need help finding a sitter.

Can I bring someone along who wasn't invited?

Unfortunately, in order to stay within the capacity of our venue we are unable to accommodate any additional guests. The people named on the invitation are the guests we have space for. Thank you for understanding!

What will the weather be like?

Bogota gets chili specially at night, temperature range from 8 °C (40 °F) to 21 °C (70 °F) bring a jacket. Pray that it doesn't rain!

Where should I park?

We recommend you Uber to the venue, in Colombia you should sit in the front seat. Cabify and Didi are other apps that work, although we recommend uber. There will be limited parking in the venue.

What should I wear?

Formal Day attire. Dresses, jumpsuits, suits, ties, flowers and colors encouraged, review the Dress Code section.

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

Our wedding ceremony is outdoors (limited indoor seating), but our reception will be partially indoors and under a tent.

Is it okay to take pictures with our phones and cameras during the wedding?

Yes! We would love for you to take photos and share them with us. Please share them with us in this link: PENDING

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony will begin promptly at 2:00 PM. Please arrive at least 30 minutes prior to the ceremony.

What time will the reception end?

The reception will end at 12:00 AM- Weddings in Colombia are longer than American weddings, we will feed you and give you plenty to drink.

Will there be vegetarian options?

Yes, we will have vegetarian options available. Please let us know in advance if you have any dietary restrictions.

Is there a hotel block for out-of-town guests?

Yes, we have reserved a block of rooms at a nearby hotel, please look at the travel section.
